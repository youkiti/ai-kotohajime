# Privacy

## We Collect No Logs

This site collects no logs whatsoever, including access analytics. It is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, with no server (backend) that receives or stores data — so structurally, it cannot collect logs.

The site's own code never sends your input or browsing activity anywhere. Note that the theme used to display the site (Material for MkDocs) includes a mechanism that, only when viewed in a very old browser, loads compatibility components from an external CDN; this does not transmit any information about your browsing activity, and it does not occur in ordinary browsers.

## About Quiz-Passing Status and Practical Exercise Submission Records

- When you pass an area quiz, and when you submit the Area B practical exercise, that record is saved to the localStorage of the browser you are using.
- What is saved is **only the pass date/time and the submission date/time, for each area**. Your name, answer content, and detailed scores are not saved.
- The feedback text you paste in for the Area B practical exercise is used within your browser only to check for the heading and character count — it is **never saved or sent anywhere**.
- Storage is confined to your own device; nothing is ever sent to any server on the site's side.
- If you want to clear your records, either press the "Clear all records on this device" button on the [certificate page](mokuroku.md), or delete the site's data from your browser settings.

## About the Name on the Certificate

The name (a nickname is fine) that you enter when issuing a certificate is used only to generate a PNG image within your browser. What you enter is never sent anywhere.

## About Input to the Feedback AI

Feedback works by copying the scoring prompt from the [Feedback AI](feedback.md) page into a generative AI of your own choosing. How your input to that generative AI is handled follows the privacy policy of whichever service you use (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.). This site does not manage that data. **Do not enter patient information or personal information.**

## About External Links

External sites linked from this site are governed by their own respective privacy policies.
