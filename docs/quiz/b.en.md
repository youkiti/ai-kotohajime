# Area B Quiz & Practical Exercise

!!! note "Issuing the Area B certificate requires two things"
    Because Area B ("Interactive AI Skills") deals with hands-on skills, issuing the certificate requires **both passing the quiz** and **submitting the practical exercise**. Either one can come first.

## Quiz Rules

- There are 5 questions in scenario format, each with four choices (read the situation and choose the best next action).
- All questions are scored together after you answer every one (batch scoring).
- You pass with 4 or more correct answers (80%).
- An explanation is shown for any question you got wrong.
- You can retry as many times as you like. There is no limit.
- You can attempt the quiz without having read [Unit B-1](../units/b1.md) or [Unit B-2](../units/b2.md).
- Your passing record is stored only in this device's browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to a server.

## Submitting the Practical Exercise

1. Using the prompt from [Unit B-1](../units/b1.md) or [Unit B-2](../units/b2.md) (any profession's version is fine), have a conversation with a generative AI.
2. At the end of that conversation, send the feedback prompt from the [Feedback AI](../feedback.md) page and receive feedback.
3. Copy the feedback you receive in full, including the first-line heading "[AI Kotohajime Feedback]", and paste it into the field below to submit it.

!!! warning "Check before pasting"
    Make sure the feedback text does not contain patient information or personal information. Note that the pasted text is used only to check for the heading and character count — **it is never sent to or stored on any server**. Only the submission date and time are saved in your browser (localStorage).
