# How to Use

## What you need

- A generative AI tool you already use: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, or similar. The free tier is fine — no paid subscription is required.
- A browser: just the browser you use to open this site. No Git or software installation is needed.
- Estimated time: about 10 minutes per unit, and 5–10 minutes for each area quiz.

## Five steps to learning

1. **Read the unit** — On each area's unit page, review why the skill matters, the core explanation, and the example prompts.
2. **Try the prompts** — Copy the ready-to-use prompts provided in the unit and paste them into the generative AI tool you already use.
3. **Get feedback from AI** — At the end of the conversation where you did the exercise, paste and send the feedback prompt from the [Feedback AI](feedback.md) page to receive feedback. In Area B, submitting this feedback is a requirement (the practical exercise) for issuing the certificate.
4. **Take the area quiz** — After finishing the units for an area, take that area's multiple-choice quiz. A score of 80% or higher is a pass (8 of 10 questions for Areas A and C, 4 of 5 for Area B). An explanation is shown for any question you get wrong.
5. **Issue your certificate** — Once you meet the issuance requirements (passing the quiz for Areas A and C; passing the quiz and submitting the practical exercise for Area B), an "Issue Certificate" button appears. Enter your name (a nickname is fine) to generate a PNG image, which you can download or share on social media.

!!! tip "If you're already experienced with generative AI"
    If you already use generative AI regularly, feel free to skip straight to the area quiz without reading the unit text. **Viewing the units is not required to issue a certificate** (Areas A and C require only passing the quiz; Area B requires passing the quiz and submitting the practical exercise).

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Is there any cost?"
    This site itself is free. The content is designed so you can fully experience it using the free tier of your generative AI tool. No paid plan is required.

??? question "Do I need to create an account?"
    No. This site has no login mechanism.

??? question "Where is my learning record stored?"
    Your quiz pass status and the submission times of your practical exercises are stored only inside your browser (localStorage). Nothing is ever sent to a server. If you switch browsers or clear your browsing history/site data, this record will be lost. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.
