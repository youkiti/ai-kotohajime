# AI Kotohajime

**A self-study site on generative AI for healthcare professionals**

!!! warning "This site is in beta"
    The site currently published is a beta version. The content has not yet undergone editorial supervision and is subject to change. You can complete the learning activities and issue a certificate for **all three areas -- A, B, and C**.

## If your supervisor or instructor sent you this link

If an attending physician or instructor sent you a link to this site, just follow the guidance on this page. No registration or account creation is required. All you need is the browser you already use and a generative AI tool you already have access to (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot), and you can get started right away.

## Three steps to get started

1. **Learn** — Read each unit and try the prompts it provides with the generative AI tool you already use. Each unit takes about 10 minutes.
2. **Take the quiz** — Take the multiple-choice quiz for each area. A score of 80% or higher is a pass, and you can retake it as many times as you like. Area B also includes a practical exercise in which you receive feedback from an AI.
3. **Issue your certificate** — Once you meet the requirements for an area, you can issue a "certificate" (*mokuroku*) on the spot. You may use a nickname instead of your real name.

For detailed steps, see [How to Use](how-to-use.md).

## The three areas

- **Area A: How Generative AI Works** — Learn how large language models and image generation work, and why hallucinations occur. [Go to Area A →](units/a1.md)
- **Area B: Interactive AI Skills** — Learn iterative refinement, how to provide context, and techniques for having AI ask questions and critique your work. [Go to Area B →](units/b1.md)
- **Area C: Ethics, Safety, and Verification** — Learn about protecting patient information, copyright, bias, and human verification. [Go to Area C →](units/c1.md)

## Our promises

- **No registration required** — No account creation or login is needed.
- **Zero log collection** — Your usage is never recorded by this site, including any form of access analytics. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.
- **The certificate is not an official credential** — It is a self-issued tool for marking your sense of accomplishment and, if you wish, reporting completion. See the [Disclaimer](disclaimer.md) for details.
