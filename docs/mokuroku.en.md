# Certificate (Mokuroku)

The "certificate" (*mokuroku*) is a completion certificate you can issue yourself once you meet the completion requirements for an area (passing the quiz; Area B also requires submitting the practical exercise). It is a self-reported tool with no verification mechanism, and **it is not an official training credential**. You may enter a nickname in the name field. The name you enter is used only to generate the image locally in your browser and is never sent anywhere. The generated image can be downloaded or shared on social media.
